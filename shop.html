Retail direction

Keep the storefront clear, useful, and aligned with the brand.

Good launch priorities

  • Start with best-selling aftercare and a tight merch selection.
  • Use strong photography and short, direct product descriptions.
  • Only offer products that match the studio's visual standard.

Useful customer info

  • Show whether pickup, delivery, or in-studio purchase is available.
  • Clarify recommended use for any healing or maintenance product.
  • Give gift cards a simple redemption path through the contact page.
