Before you book

Come in prepared so the session time goes into the work itself.

Consultation essentials

  • Bring reference images, placement ideas, and size expectations.
  • Wear clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed.
  • Eat beforehand and stay hydrated before your appointment.

Aftercare priority

  • Follow the artist's cleaning and moisturizing instructions exactly.
  • Avoid pools, friction, and excess sun exposure during healing.
  • Reach out early if you need guidance instead of guessing mid-heal.
