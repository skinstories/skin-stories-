What to expect

Clean steps, no guesswork, and no rushed decision-making.

Appointment flow

  • Discuss placement and jewelry based on anatomy and healing time.
  • Mark and confirm the placement before any piercing starts.
  • Leave with aftercare instructions that match the actual piercing performed.

Before you arrive

  • Bring valid ID and confirm any age or consent requirements in advance.
  • Avoid touching or irritating the area right before the visit.
  • Plan for healing time and jewelry care after the appointment.
